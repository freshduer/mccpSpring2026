Presentation 2 – Poster preparation and schedule

Please do the following before the poster presentation sessions.

1. Sync your GitHub repo with Simon’s repo

Sync your GitHub repository with Simon’s course repo so you have the latest materials. Then pull the updates locally so your pre2 folder is up to date.

2. Review the pre2 folder

Review the pre2 folder, especially:

  • mccpSpring2026/pre2/demo — poster design inspiration, institutional guidelines, and posterScripts (templates, export tools, insights from several poster repos).
  • mccpSpring2026/pre2/sample — a full example: samplePoster.html (poster draft in HTML), sampleProcess.md (process and requirement alignment), and outlineScript.md (presentation outline and script). Use these as a reference for structure and workflow.

3. Workflow for your poster

Use this workflow to prepare your poster and presentation:

  1. Prepare a poster draft in HTML (you can start from the sample or from templates in pre2/demo/posterScripts).
  2. Prepare an outline and/or script in Markdown (e.g. outlineScript.md) for your 2–3 minute presentation.
  3. Give feedback to an AI agent (e.g. in Cursor or another tool) to revise both the poster HTML and the outline/script MD. Iterate as needed.

Ensure your poster meets the course and institutional requirements (see materials in pre2 and the sample’s sampleProcess.md for alignment).

4. Presentation dates

9 March and 16 March — Students are expected to present their posters, followed by Q&A sessions. Please have your poster and script ready and be prepared to present and answer questions.

If you have any questions, ask in class or via the usual channels.
