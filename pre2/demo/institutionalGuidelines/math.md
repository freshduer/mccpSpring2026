Hong Kong Baptist UniversityDepartment of MathematicsGuidelines for Poster Presentation The Poster Presentation provides an opportunity for RPg students to showcase their scientific research, foster intellectual exchange, and engage both specialist and non-specialist audiences through clear and visually compelling posters. Presenters are required to prepare an A0 poster adhering to the guidelines below:1.Poster GuidelinesDimension: A0 (841 mm x 1189 mm, portrait orientation).File Format: PDF (600 dpi resolution, file size <25 MB). Content: oLogo Placement: HKBU logo (top left); Department of Mathematics (top right). oHeader: Research project title, presenter’s name, and Principal Supervisor’s name (centred). oSections: Introduction, Methodology, Results, Discussion/Conclusion, and References. oLayman’s summary: A concise and non-technical overview. Fonts: oTitles/Headings: Sans-serif fonts (e.g., Arial, Franklin Gothic, Helvetica, Tahoma, Trebuchet or Verdana), ≥100 pt. 【字号需要在PDF上测量调整】oBody Text: Serif fonts (e.g., Times New Roman or Palatino), ≥24 pt (or ≥30 pt recommended for readability from 1.8 metres). oCapitalisation: Use sentence case (avoid all CAPS). oAdjust font sizes for optimal visual balance and readability from 1–2 
metres. Design: oAccessibility: Ensure the poster is comprehensible to non-specialists, using minimal text and clear visuals to convey research significance and impact.oText-to-Visual Balance: Text (50–60%) and visuals (40–50%, e.g., graphs, images with captions). oGraphs and Tables: Minimum size 216 x 280 mm.  Delivery: oPresenters must stand by their posters during the Poster Exhibition to present their research and answer questions from visitors. 2.Optional ResourcesPresenters need not follow the provided template exactly, but their posters should be clear and self-explanatory. Recommended resources: Creating Posters with PowerPointDesign an A1 Poster using PowerPointPoster-making in PowerPointPoster Template Poster Design Principles & Tips3.Submission & PrintingUpload the PDF poster with Principal Supervisor’s endorsement to OneDrive. Send the shareable link to Prof. Andrew Lam (akflam@hkbu.edu.hk) and Candy Li (candyli@hkbu.edu.hk), copying your Principal Supervisor, by Wednesday, 17 December 2025. Upon submission, the Department will arrange printing of the poster and cover the costs.
